A week-long adventure plan exploring some of the best hiking and climbing routes in the North Cascades and Mount Rainier area.

## Itinerary

### Days 1-2: North Cascades
Base camp near Marblemount and tackle the Blue Lake Trail for acclimatization and warm-up.

### Days 3-4: Rest and Travel
Recovery day and travel to Mount Rainier National Park.

### Days 5-7: Mount Rainier
Attempt the Disappointment Cleaver route with an overnight at Camp Muir.

## Logistics

- Permits required for both areas
- Weather contingency plans
- Gear resupply at Paradise
