A beautiful day hike to pristine Blue Lake in the North Cascades. This moderate trail offers stunning alpine scenery and crystal-clear mountain lake views.

## Route Description

The trail begins at the Blue Lake trailhead and follows a well-maintained path through old-growth forest before ascending into alpine terrain. The final approach to the lake involves some scrambling over granite slabs.

## Best Time to Visit

Late July through September when the snow has melted and the wildflowers are in bloom.

## Gear Recommendations

- Sturdy hiking boots
- Trekking poles
- Layers for changing weather
- Plenty of water and snacks
