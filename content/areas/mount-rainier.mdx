Mount Rainier National Park offers world-class mountaineering and hiking opportunities in Washington State. The massive stratovolcano dominates the landscape at 14,411 feet, surrounded by pristine wilderness areas.

## Overview

The park features extensive trail systems, from day hikes through old-growth forests to multi-day alpine climbs. Popular routes include the Wonderland Trail, various summit routes, and numerous day hiking options.

## Access

The park has multiple entrances with the most popular being Longmire, Paradise, and Sunrise. Check current conditions as many high-elevation areas are snow-covered from October through June.
