The North Cascades National Park is a stunning area featuring rugged peaks, pristine alpine lakes, and challenging climbing routes. Known as the "American Alps," this region offers some of the most spectacular mountain scenery in the continental United States.

## Overview

The park encompasses over 504,000 acres of wilderness, including dramatic glaciated peaks, old-growth forests, and hundreds of lakes and waterfalls. The area is popular among climbers, hikers, and mountaineers seeking challenging adventures in a pristine alpine environment.

## Access

Access to most climbing areas requires hiking on well-maintained trails. The nearest town is Marblemount, which provides basic services including groceries and lodging.
